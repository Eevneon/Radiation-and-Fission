# A long but non-final list of planned features:

### The Centrifuge:
In the centrifuge, you put goopanium fluid - yes I called it that, because it'll be slime balls + uranium + water + maybe something else. Then, you'd power the centrifuge and get different sections in a new kind of fluid tank block. This fluid tank block would be right-clicked with membranes to configure the amount of layers you want. These layers would then separate the products from those recipes, because materials like uranium would be separated into different enrichment levels. These uranium enrichment levels will be integer only, to keep it simple. Based on the speed input of the centrifuge and the density, volume and viscosity of the initial fluid, the different layers will separate into the recipe products.
