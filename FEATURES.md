# Create: Radiation & Fission
> Made by Eevneon
### An extensive list of features present in Create: Radiation & Fission
> Contains only features that are implemented in the mod. Anything that's not in this file already can't be guaranteed to be featured in any versions of Radiation & Fission

**Ingredients:**
- Uranium Ingot (a)
- Block of Uranium (a)
- Steel Ingot
- Steel Block
- Steel Casing (c)
> Steel Casing has connected textures
(a): animated textures
(c): connected textures

**Functional Blocks:**
- Centrifuge Bearing:
The base for the centrifuge to be built on. (See "The Centrifuge"). 
- Powered Speed Controller:
An extremely smart, customizable kinetic redstone component that varies rotational speed by following a list of instructions, similar but not equal to the sequenced gearshift. The Powered Speed Controller will also allow the player to regulate the amount of SU throughput to the other side of the block. This block will allow you to isolate areas of high-stress in nuclear powerplants and other high-stress environments such that the more essential areas will keep running while you fix the overstressed section.

**Contraptions:**
- The Centrifuge:
A spinning contraption consisting of membrane-separated chambered fluid tanks (name a work in progress) horizontally mounted on the side of a central pillar. When spinning this bad boy around, the more massive substances move to the chambers that are furthest away from the central pillar. This process is able to split enriched uranium from natural uranium (U-235 from U-238 for the Nuclear Obsessives among us).
This contraption interacts with the world via portable interfaces, which now only extend while the contraption isn't running. Disassembling the centrifuge while the fluid tanks are filled will remove or "spill" any fluid contents from the contraption.
